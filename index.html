Independent. Boutique. Accountable.

Three things every wirehouse cannot offer you

The largest names in wealth management answer to shareholders, sales quotas, and proprietary product shelves. Aksala answers to one constituency.

True fiduciary, every account

As a registered investment adviser, Aksala is legally obligated to act in your best interest on every recommendation, every quarter, with every dollar. No commission products. No proprietary fund shelf. No conflict to disclose because the conflict does not exist.

Micro family office service

Investment management, tax-intelligent planning, estate coordination, and family governance under one relationship. Your CPA and estate attorney are not afterthoughts; they are integrated participants in the planning process.

One family at a time

Boutique by design. The team works with a finite number of households so each receives senior-advisor attention. When you call, you reach a person who knows your situation in detail, not a rotating call center.

Services

Coordinated wealth planning for life's biggest transitions

Whether you are preparing to sell a business, relocating to Florida for retirement, or thinking about how your wealth passes to the next generation, the planning challenge is integration. Aksala specializes in the integration.

Retirement Transition Planning

For pre-retirees and retirees relocating to Florida or preparing for the shift from earning to spending. Domicile establishment, homestead exemption, estate plan updates aligned with Florida law, and income portfolio construction.

Business Exit and Liquidity Events

For founders and operators selling companies in the $5M to $50M range. Pre-sale tax mitigation, entity structuring, deferred sale strategies, and the implementation of multigenerational trusts post-close.

Multigenerational Wealth and Family Office

For families coordinating wealth across three or more generations. Family governance, education for inheritors, philanthropy strategy, and integration of advisors who serve different generations under a single coordinated plan.

Investment Management with Rising-Income Focus

Portfolios built around income streams that can grow with inflation, not just preserve principal. Core strategy combines dividend-growth equities, tax-managed positioning, and structured income solutions calibrated to your withdrawal needs.

How We Work

The four-meeting planning process

Wealth planning is a sequence, not a single conversation. Each step builds on the last with no surprises and no high-pressure decisions.

01

Exploration

We start by getting to know you. Your values, goals, and financial picture. We determine mutual fit before anything else moves forward.

02

Recommendation

A customized strategy presented in plain language. Aligned to your goals and life stage, with tax-intelligent planning at the foundation.

03

Implementation

Account openings, asset transfers, and execution handled by the team. You stay informed through every transition step.

04

Lifestyle Check-up

Portfolio reviewed regularly. We adjust for market conditions, tax-law changes, and the things that change in your life.
